Welcome to expressRNA
Interactive RNA Bioinformatics Platform
The project started in 2013 when a platform was needed to share results of RNA post-transcriptional analyses with scientific labs around the world. Most work was centered on alternative polyadenylation (APA) of diverse RNA samples sequenced with 3'-end targeted protocols. Recently, expressRNA evolved to process long-read Nanopore reads, motif search and alternative splicing analyses. Regulatory information is elucidated with RNA-Maps that integrate RNA-Protein Binding *CLIP data disclosing how RNA binding positions of proteins relate to the regulatory functions.
