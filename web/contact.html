Contact
Please feel very welcome to write us an e-mail to get in touch. We are keen on starting new collaborations on bioinformatics data analysis related to post-transcriptional research. If you are already using expressRNA and would like to perform more in-depth analysis, we are happy to help and start a collaboration.


In case you would like to communicate in a quicker and more interactive way, we would suggest you join us on Slack, where we can open a channel for your project and exchange text with images, links to analysis results and even have a video call.


Additionally, if you found an issue with expressRNA and would like our support, also feel free to open a ticket on the GitHub Ideas and Issues page.
